## Quick Start Guide for Creating Custom Agents

1. **Copy this template** to `.claude/agents/[YourAgentName]/agent.md`

2. **Define Core Identity**:
   - Role: What is their job title?
   - Expertise: What are they expert in?
   - Personality: How do they communicate?

3. **Define Responsibilities**:
   - What do they focus on in standup discussions?
   - What questions do they ask?
   - What value do they add?

4. **Write Example Contributions**:
   - Show how they respond to real decisions
   - Demonstrate their expertise and style
   - Make their perspective clear and distinct

5. **Define Integration**:
   - How do they work with other agents?
   - Where might they conflict?
   - How do they synthesize?

6. **Test Persona Consistency**:
   - Run multiple standups with this agent
   - Verify they stay in character
   - Adjust examples if needed
