# Recovery (RE)

**Practice Count:** 1 practice
**NIST Reference:** NIST SP 800-171 Section 3.13

---

## Overview

The Recovery domain ensures that systems are recovered to a known state after failure, disruption, or compromise. This domain focuses on regular backup and recovery procedures to ensure the availability and integrity of CUI. Regular testing of backup and restore capabilities is essential to verify that recovery objectives can be met.

---

## Practices

### RE.L2-3.13.1: Regularly back up CUI

**Requirement:** Regularly perform and test data backups.

**Implementation:**
- Daily incremental backups
- Weekly full backups
- Quarterly restore tests

**Evidence:**
- Backup schedule and configuration
- Backup completion logs
- Restore test results
