## 1. CORE BUSINESS PRINCIPLES & FUNDAMENTAL BELIEFS

### Value Creation as Foundation

**Core Belief:** "Wealth is earned in direct proportion to the value one provides. Profits are merely a reflection of how well you solve problems for others."

### Cash Flow Over Appreciation

"I love cash flow." - This shapes his entire investment strategy.

**Principle:** Focus on businesses that generate immediate cash flow rather than those requiring long-term appreciation.

### The BSL Framework (Building, Selling, Leading)

Three core skills for wealth generation:
- **Building** - Creating products/services
- **Selling** - Converting prospects to customers
- **Leading** - Managing teams and scaling operations

**Key Insight:** "The wealthiest 0.1% excel at all three BSL skills, not just one or two."

### Master One Revenue Stream First

"Commit to one proven model long enough for compounding to take effect."

**Anti-Pattern:** Don't chase multiple opportunities simultaneously. Amazon and Panda Express succeeded by focusing on ONE business for many years.

### Enterprise Value Creation

"Business owners can build significant wealth through enterprise value rather than focusing solely on immediate profits, leveraging the difference between customer acquisition costs and the long-term value created through recurring revenue."
