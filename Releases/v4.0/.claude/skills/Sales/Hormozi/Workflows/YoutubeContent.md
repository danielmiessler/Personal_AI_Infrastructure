## üéØ CONTENT STRATEGY

### Twitter-First Testing Approach

**The Process:**
1. Fire from the hip on Twitter (rapid posting of ideas)
2. Team collects best-performing tweets (track engagement metrics)
3. Turn top performers into short-form video scripts
4. Refine and test in video format
5. Distribute across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok

**Why It Works:**
- Twitter provides instant feedback on ideas
- Lowest cost testing platform (text only)
- Best content naturally rises to top through engagement
- Video scripts built on proven concepts

### The 93-7 Principle

**Time Allocation:**
- **7% of time:** Personal content creation (2 days/month)
- **93% outsourced:** Team of editors handles production, distribution, repurposing

**Investment:**
- $20,000/month in production costs
- 2 days/month of Hormozi's personal time
- Team manages 80 posts per week across all platforms

**Key Insight:** "Dedicate only 7% of time to content creation, outsource 93% to a team" - enables massive volume with minimal founder time.

### High-Volume Production

**Output:** 80 posts per week across all platforms

**Distribution Strategy:**
1. Create one long-form video (10-30 minutes)
2. Extract 10-15 short clips (30-90 seconds each)
3. Create quote graphics (5-10)
4. Write threads/posts for each platform
5. Turn into email newsletter
6. Transcribe into blog post

**Result:** 1 hour of content creation ‚Üí 30+ pieces of content across all platforms

## üé® CONTENT FORMAT & STRUCTURE

### The Proof-Promise-Plan Framework

**Every Video Follows This Structure:**

1. **Proof:** Demonstrate you've done what you teach (credentials, results)
   - Example: "I've built X businesses to $XXM"
   - Shows self-identified credentials from start

2. **Promise:** Articulate what the content will deliver
   - Example: "By the end, you'll know exactly how to..."
   - Sets clear expectations

3. **Plan:** Deliver the actual valuable content (the teaching)
   - Detailed frameworks and tactics
   - Step-by-step processes
   - Real examples and case studies
