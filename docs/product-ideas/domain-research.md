# Context Management Product - Domain Research

> Brainstorming session: December 4, 2025

## Product Vision

Building on the Personal AI Infrastructure (PAI) context management system - a unified capture and retrieval layer for personal knowledge management powered by AI.

### Core Value Proposition

- **Unified Capture**: Telegram as universal input API for voice, text, URLs, documents, photos
- **Intelligent Processing**: AI-driven routing, transcription, summarization, and tagging
- **Semantic Retrieval**: Vector-based search across your entire knowledge base
- **Privacy-First**: Self-hosted, local-first architecture

## Naming Strategy Analysis

### What Works

1. **Action + Container**: "drop" + "cortex/brain/vault"
2. **Flow metaphors**: "pipe", "pour", "flow", "sync"
3. **Intelligence references**: "cortex", "neuro", "synapse", "cerebro"
4. **Short, memorable**: 2-word combinations max

### Brand Positioning Options

| Name | Positioning | Tagline | Target |
|------|-------------|---------|--------|
| **CortexDrop** | Your AI-powered second brain | *"Drop ideas into your cortex"* | Knowledge workers, PKM enthusiasts |
| **RecallBoost** | IQ as a Service (IQaaS) | *"Boost your recall with AI"* | Professionals needing cognitive edge |
| **MemoryBoost** | Your AI memory layer | *"Never forget, always find"* | Busy professionals, students |
| **ThinkBoost** | Cognitive enhancement | *"Think better, think faster"* | Productivity enthusiasts |
| **BrainPour** | Effortless knowledge capture | *"Pour ideas, retrieve wisdom"* | Casual, approachable users |

#### IQaaS Concept üß†

The **"IQ as a Service"** positioning is compelling:
- **Problem**: Information overload, can't remember everything, context switching
- **Promise**: *"Boost your effective IQ by giving AI access to everything you've ever captured"*
- **Differentiator**: Not just note-taking, but *cognitive augmentation*

**RecallBoost.ai** best captures this:
- "Recall" = memory retrieval (the core value)
- "Boost" = enhancement, improvement, upgrade
- Together: *"Boost your recall ability"* - exactly what the product does

## Technical Foundation

The product builds on existing PAI infrastructure:

- **Ingestion Pipeline (v2)**: Two-layer processing for multi-modal content
- **CLI Tools**: `ingest` for capture, `obs` for vault operations
- **Semantic Search**: OpenAI embeddings + sqlite-vec
- **Fabric Integration**: Content processing patterns
- **Obsidian Vault**: Primary knowledge repository

See: `docs/architecture/ingest-pipeline-v2.md` for technical details.
